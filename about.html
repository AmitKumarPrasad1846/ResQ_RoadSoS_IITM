India reports the highest number of road accident fatalities in the world. More than half of these deaths occur in the "golden window" after the crash — when a trauma centre or ambulance is nearby but no one can locate it in time. ResQ closes that gap.

Why We Built

ResQ was built for all of them. One tap. One location. One dispatch.
